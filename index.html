Our Story

A Legacy of Purity Since 2009

Labanya Milk was born from a simple belief — every family deserves access to pure, unadulterated milk. What started as a small dairy farm has grown into one of the most trusted milk brands in the region.

Our cattle are raised with love on lush green pastures, fed natural fodder, and cared for by experienced dairy farmers. We follow a strict no-additive policy, ensuring that what reaches your glass is nothing but pure, creamy goodness.
