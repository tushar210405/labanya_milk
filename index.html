About Us

Best Quality Milk
One promise.

Founded in 2020, Labanya Milk began as a Milk ATM with a mission to provide fresh and pure milk from farms to customers. Today, we proudly offer doorstep milk delivery across CDA to more than 1k customers. We also supply fresh milk throughout Cuttack city, along with direct availability at our store. At Labanya Milk, we are committed to quality, freshness, and reliable service for every household. We are looking forward to you joining our family.
